# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be decent. Argue with the ideas as hard as you like; do not go after the person holding them.

## What is welcome

Blunt technical disagreement. "This is wrong and here is why" is a contribution, not an attack, and
this project would rather be corrected than flattered. If you think a chapter is naive, say so.

## What is not

Personal attacks, harassment, demeaning comments, or any behavior that makes someone hesitate
before posting a question. Sustained or deliberate disruption after being asked to stop.

Someone asking a beginner question is exactly who this series is written for. Treat them
accordingly.

## Scope

This applies in issues, pull requests, and discussions on this repository, and to anyone
representing the project elsewhere.

## Enforcement

Report anything that needs attention to **info@nugalaxy.ai**. Reports stay private. Maintainers
will edit, hide, or delete comments that violate this document, and may block accounts for
repeated or severe behavior.
